## 16. FAQ Section

**Q: Is this app only for SOK Academy students?**  
A: No! While optimized for SOK Academy's methodology, it works for any student of knowledge - whether you study with Garden of Ilm, local institutes, online platforms, or independently.

**Q: Can I modify the default schedules?**  
A: Absolutely! The profiles provide starting points. Customize everything to fit your needs.

**Q: What if my schedule changes (e.g., Ramadan, life events)?**  
A: Switch profiles temporarily or adjust your activities. The app adapts to you.

**Q: Can sisters use this app?**  
A: Yes! The "Rabbat al-Usra" profile is specifically designed for sisters managing home and family, but all profiles are for both men and women.

**Q: How do I know if I'm balancing subjects correctly?**  
A: Check your weekly analytics. Aim for roughly 40-50% tool sciences and 40-50% core sciences, with 10-20% revision/practice.

**Q: Should I finish one book before starting another?**  
A: Not necessarily. Many students study 2-4 subjects simultaneously. This keeps variety and prevents monotony.

**Q: What about Arabic and Qur'an?**  
A: Include them daily! Qur'an revision should be non-negotiable. Arabic can be a regular lesson or daily practice.

**Q: Can I use this for children's Islamic education?**  
A: The current version is designed for adult/teen students. You could adapt it, but consider age-appropriate modifications.

**Q: Does this replace my teacher?**  
A: Never! This organizes your self-study. Always refer questions to qualified scholars and maintain connection with teachers.

**Q: What if I fall behind?**  
A: Don't try to "catch up" by cramming. Just resume your normal schedule. Use revision time to revisit any missed content naturally.
